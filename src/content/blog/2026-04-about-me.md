My name is Daniel Olusheki, and I'm a Nigerian American from Queens, New York, currently attending Brandeis University as a double major in computer science and biology. I want this to be a more informal introduction to me, so here are some things about me.

![A photo of me](/me.jpeg)

As hobbies, I like to do a lot of creative things like drawing, sewing, and playing guitar. Charcoal and graphite are my main mediums for drawing. I like to upcycle jeans and post my work on an Instagram account [@shekisewn](https://www.instagram.com/shekisewn/). I mostly play samba and bossa nova songs on guitar, and I'm working on my fingerstyle songs. A song that I'm currently learning is [All The Things You Are by Baden Powell](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BY-iTjhmXFM).

If my friends had to describe me, they might say these things: I'm funny, optimistic, and kind. I'm kind of introverted, but I speak when I have something to say.
